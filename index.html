THE PROBLEM

Groceries in the fridge. No idea what to cook.

Most people don't lack ingredients ‚Äî they lack the bridge from raw groceries to a finished meal. The result is wasted food, wasted money, and another night of instant noodles or takeout. Existing apps solve fragments of the cooking journey, but none connect inventory to recipe to guidance in one flow.

Tasty

Entertaining recipes, but browsing-first ‚Äî no personalization to what you already own.

Supercook

Searches by ingredient, but limited AI and poor mobile experience.

Mealime

Strong recipe-to-grocery flow, but starts with recipes ‚Äî not your fridge.

MyFitnessPal

Gold standard for nutrition, but entirely disconnected from actual cooking.


CORE FLOW

From grocery list to dinner table.

Nourish inverts the typical recipe app model. Instead of starting with a recipe and building a shopping list, we start with what's already in your kitchen and generate a recipe you can actually cook tonight.

1


Input your groceries

Quick manual entry with fuzzy-match autocomplete across 1,000+ ingredients. Your kitchen inventory persists across sessions.

2


Set your preferences

Dietary restrictions, skill level, time budget, servings, and meal type. Saved to your profile ‚Äî set once, used every time.

3


Generate a recipe with AI

One tap. Claude generates a structured, skill-appropriate recipe with ingredients, nutrition, and integrated timer data ‚Äî in under 5 seconds.

4


Cook with guided steps

Full-screen cooking mode with large text, step-by-step navigation, integrated countdown timers, and keep-screen-awake ‚Äî designed for messy hands at arm's length.

5

Rate, save, and cook again

Rate recipes, save favorites to your cookbook, and browse your full history. Every recipe is stored so you never lose one.
